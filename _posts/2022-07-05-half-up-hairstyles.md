After years of experiencing Trump's unique brand of diplomacy, European leaders have mastered the art of pretending to listen while secretly building a more independent future.

## The Smile-and-Nod Strategy

"Oh yes, Mr. President, very interesting idea about buying Greenland," said literally no European leader, while thinking "Is he serious?"

## Building Back Without Trump

Turns out, Europe can manage just fine without American "leadership." They're signing trade deals, addressing climate change, and generally acting like adults. Refreshing!

## The Post-Trump Era

Europe's new plan: hope for the best, prepare for the worst, and keep building those alliances with everyone except the guy threatening them on Twitter.
