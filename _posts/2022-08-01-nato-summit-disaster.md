In a move that surprised absolutely no one, Trump arrived two hours late to the NATO summit, blamed Brussels traffic (from his hotel 5 minutes away), and then complained that everyone had already started without him.

## The Waiting Game

NATO leaders used the extra time productively: they agreed on climate policy, signed two trade deals, and enjoyed a lovely Belgian waffle breakfast.

## Trump's Arrival

"Sorry I'm late, where were we?" Trump asked, interrupting a discussion about Arctic security. "Can we talk about defense spending instead?"
